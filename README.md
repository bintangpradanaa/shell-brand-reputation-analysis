## Project Overview
In February 2025, a fuel adulteration case involving Pertamina was revealed, where some stations mixed lower-quality fuel with higher-grade products. This issue reduced public trust and triggered widespread discussion about fuel quality in Indonesia.

As a **Data Analyst at Shell Indonesia**, I was tasked with analyzing public conversations to understand sentiment, emotions, and key discussion topics related to Shell during this period. The goal is to understand public perception toward Shell Indonesia's brand reputation during the issue to translate these insights into strategic recommendations.

## Objectives
- Analyze public perception toward Shell  
- Identify emotions and key discussion topics  
- Support strategic decision-making  

## Data
- Source: Platform X (Twitter)  
- Time Range: Feb 28 - Mar 18, 2025  
- Size: 215 rows, 6 columns  

## Tools & Methods
- Python  
- Sentiment Analysis (RoBERTa)  
- Emotion Detection (RoBERTa)  
- Topic Modeling (BERTopic)  

## Key Insights
- Sentiment toward Shell is predominantly positive, indicating a generally favorable brand perception. However, the presence of negative sentiment suggests ongoing public scrutiny and sensitivity toward fuel-related issues
- Shell is more strongly associated with positive emotions such as happiness, reinforcing its relatively strong brand positioning, although the persistence of anger and fear highlights underlying concerns within the industry. In comparison, Pertamina is more heavily associated with negative emotions, particularly anger
- Public discussions are largely centered on Public Reaction Fuel Issue, Station Operations Issues, and Fuel Price, Quality, & Usage Experience Comparison, suggesting that brand perception is highly influenced by functional performance and service experience.

## Recommendations
- Improve customer experience at fuel stations (handling long queues, payment, and fueling process)  
- Increase transparency regarding fuel quality differences
- Strengthen communication during operational disruptions  
- Reinforce positive brand perception through targeted messaging  
- Optimize fuel availability in high-demand locations
